Creme Brulee

I love this recipe because it is so easy to make an it feels expensive even though it's not

Ingredients

    5 Eggs
    2 cups (450 ml) heavy cream
    ½ tsp (4 g) kosher salt
    2 tsp (6 g)vanilla extract
    1/2 cup (100 g) granulated sugar

Instructions

    Crack the eggs into a bowl then separate the yolks into another bowl
    Add the heavy cream, salt, and vanilla extract to a saucepang then mix over medium heat until hot and steaming
    Beat the egg yolks while gradually adding in the sugar until all of it is mixed in
    Splash a bit of the hot mixture into egg yolks, stir until incorporated. Repeat until eggs mixture is hot
    Add the egg mixture into the cream mixture until combined
    In four 6” ramekins set into a baking dish, evenly divide the creme brûlée into each ramekin. Fill baking dish (not ramekins) with boiling water until ¾ of the way up the ramekins.
    Bake in a preheated oven set to 325F for 30-40 minutes
    Remove from the oven and hot bath. Place ramekin onto a wire rack to cool completely
    Once cooled wrap in plastic wrap and put in the fridge for at least 4 hours
    To server, springkle on sugar then melt it with a blow torch until you see some char
